## Mississippi Sports Group Launches Mississippi Sports App and MississippiSportsApp.com — Delivering Statewide High School and College Sports Coverage to Fans Across the Magnolia State

**MISSISSIPPI — February 21, 2026** — Mississippi Sports Group, a trusted name in Mississippi sports journalism since 2006, today announced the official launch of the **Mississippi Sports app** for iOS and the companion website **MississippiSportsApp.com**, giving Mississippi sports fans a single destination for high school and college sports news, scores, and coverage from across the state.

The free Mississippi Sports app is now available for download on the [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/mississippi-sports/id6758892239).

### Statewide Mississippi Sports Coverage in One App

The Mississippi Sports app aggregates sports news, scores, and stories from multiple Mississippi sports publications into one unified experience. From Friday night high school football in Tippah County and DeSoto County to NCAA basketball, baseball, and football matchups at Mississippi's colleges and universities, the app delivers the coverage Mississippi sports fans have been waiting for.

"Mississippi has some of the most passionate sports communities in the country, and they deserve a dedicated platform that brings it all together," said **Jon Ross Myers**, founder of Mississippi Sports Group. "Whether it's high school football scores from North Mississippi, college basketball previews, or breaking recruitment news, the Mississippi Sports app puts it all in the palm of your hand."

### Key Features of the Mississippi Sports App

- **Aggregated News Feed** — Real-time sports news from across Mississippi, updated throughout the day
- **High School Sports Coverage** — Scores, standings, and stories from Mississippi high school athletics, including football, basketball, baseball, softball, and more
- **College Sports Coverage** — In-depth coverage of Mississippi State, Ole Miss, Southern Miss, Jackson State, Alcorn State, Mississippi Valley State, and other Mississippi colleges and universities
- **Multi-County Reach** — Coverage spanning Tippah County, DeSoto County, Alcorn County, Prentiss County, Union County, and communities statewide
- **Free to Download** — No subscription required

### MississippiSportsApp.com — The Digital Hub for Mississippi Sports

Alongside the app launch, Mississippi Sports Group has launched **MississippiSportsApp.com**, a fast, mobile-first website featuring live RSS-aggregated sports news from the group's growing network of Mississippi sports publications. The website includes:

- A real-time aggregated sports news feed from six Mississippi sports publications
- Direct download links for the Mississippi Sports app
- Newsletter subscription for daily Mississippi sports updates
- Contact information for tips, story submissions, and advertising inquiries

The website was built on a modern, performance-optimized tech stack and is hosted on Cloudflare's global edge network, ensuring fast load times for users across Mississippi and beyond.

### About Mississippi Sports Group

Mississippi Sports Group was founded in 2006 by veteran Mississippi sports journalist **Jon Ross Myers**. What began as a single community sports publication has grown into a statewide digital network covering high school and college athletics across Mississippi.

The Mississippi Sports Group network includes:

- **Sports Mississippi** (SportsMississippi.com) — Statewide Mississippi sports news and coverage
- **Tippah Sports** (TippahSports.com) — Tippah County high school and community sports
- **Alcorn Sports MS** (AlcornSportsMS.com) — Alcorn County sports coverage
- **Prentiss Sports MS** (PrentissSportsMS.com) — Prentiss County sports coverage
- **Union Sports MS** (UnionSportsMS.com) — Union County sports coverage
- **DeSoto County News** (DeSotoCountyNews.com) — DeSoto County sports and community news

Mississippi Sports Group is committed to trusted, local sports journalism and building the future of sports media in Mississippi.
